What are Lithophanes?

iPhane lithophanes are 3D printed photos where the photo becomes visible when held up to the light. Lithophanes look great hung on windows, set on windowsills, or mounted on lampshades, and make great gifts for any occasion.

Send us a photo you want to have turned into a Lithophane and we can turn it into reality!

About us

Why do we make Lithophanes?

Originally we were looking for ways to make photos tactile for the benefit of the blind. Along the way we stumbled into the art of making lithophanes, sculptures that show images when held up to the light. Everyone we've shown them to has been impressed and wanted to know how they could get one themselves, thus iPhane was born.
